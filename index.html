旗艦模組

AI HR.Interview
系統設計思維

面向半導體業的候選人端 AI 語音面試系統，將面試流程標準化，並自動產出結構化評估結果供 HR 與主管審核。以下是三個核心設計決策，每一個都來自 HR 實務的洞察。

01

授權機制

JWT 專屬連結 + 到期機制，而非帳號註冊

針對特定候選人在面試階段產生專屬連結，並設有到期時間，確保連結僅在有效期內使用、避免濫用或轉發。候選人點擊即進入，體驗接近「收到面試通知、準時出現」的直覺，不增加任何額外操作負擔。

02

評估設計

多模態行為分析，而非單一維度評估

結合表情、聲學特徵、肢體姿態三個維度，並追蹤全程情緒走勢——例如在壓力題出現時候選人的反應變化。讓評估更接近真實面試官的綜合判斷，同時降低單一訊號的誤判風險。

03

協作流程

結果寫回後台 + 主管協作，而非寄送報告

面試結果自動寫回 HR 後台，HR 與主管皆可在同一平台查看候選人資訊與面試狀態。主管可直接在後台與 AI Recruiter 討論，決策在既有工作流程中完成，不需切換工具。

AI HR.Interview
System Design Philosophy

This is the one candidates actually go through. It turns a semiconductor company's interview process into something standardized and repeatable, and hands HR and hiring managers a structured report the moment it's done. Three decisions shaped how it works — each one came out of something I ran into firsthand in HR.

01

Authorization Mechanism

A Link That Expires, Instead of an Account to Create

No sign-up, no password. Each candidate gets a unique link, valid only for their interview window, so it can't be reused or passed along after the fact. Click it, and the interview starts — it should feel like showing up for an appointment, not registering for a service.

02

Evaluation Design

Reading the Whole Candidate, Not Just Their Answers

The system watches expression, voice, and posture together, and tracks how each one shifts over the course of the interview — especially the moment a stress question lands. Any single signal can be read wrong on its own. Combining three gets closer to how an experienced interviewer actually sizes someone up.

03

Collaboration Workflow

Landing in the HR Dashboard, Not Someone's Inbox

Results write straight into the HR backend the second the interview ends, so HR and the hiring manager are looking at the same screen — not a PDF someone has to forward. Managers can even talk it over with the AI Recruiter right there, so the decision happens where the work already lives.
